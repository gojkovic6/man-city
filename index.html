Manchester City FC, commonly known as Man City, is a professional football club based in Manchester, England. Established in 1880, the club has a rich history and is one of the top football clubs in the world.Manchester City's fanbase, often referred to as the "Cityzens," is known for its passion and loyalty.In addition to domestic glory, Manchester City has also made a strong impact on the European stage, regularly competing in the UEFA Champions League.
