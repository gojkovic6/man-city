About the Club

History

Founded in 1880 as St. Mark's (West Gorton), Manchester City has undergone several name changes and relocations before settling at the Etihad Stadium. Notable periods include the 1960s when they won the league title, and the recent success under Sheikh Mansour's ownership.


Stadium

The Etihad Stadium, located in Manchester, is the home of Manchester City. With a seating capacity of over 53,000, it provides a state-of-the-art facility for fans to support their team. The stadium's modern design and atmosphere contribute to the club's success.

Fanbase

Manchester City's fanbase, often referred to as the "Cityzens," is known for its passion and loyalty. The club has a global following, with supporters from various parts of the world. The "Blue Moon" anthem echoes through the Etihad, creating an electric atmosphere on matchdays.
